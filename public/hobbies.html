My Hobbies

I am a sports enthusiast and I do like running, cycling, hiking, mountaineering, and skiing. I tried myself also in sport
climbing and I loved it. Climbing is a team sport and at these heights you really must trust each other. As I like being
on solid ground, I also like being in and on the water. Accordingly, I got my Boat Leader's License of Competence (up to 24m)
and License of Competence for VHF GMDSS Station. Following this I joined myself to a team of 5 and we sailed from Cherbourg
in Normandy, France all the way to Lisbon the capital of Portugal. That was really a unique experience for me. A voyage of
roughly 1000 nautical miles including Bay of Biscay crossing. So, a true-blue water leg of sailing. Not to mention that before
I did only a shy 100 miles of sailing in the Adriatic Sea. Did I mention that I like to travel, get to know other people and
their culture, and admire all our planet Earth has to offer.

As much as I like being physically in movement, I also like to keep my brains content and feed them by all the good staff books
can provide, learning new soft and hard skills. What "food" is better for brains than learning new languages or programing?

I really look forward to learn how to design and build a webpage from scratch!
